5 concepts of Usability!

Usability in UI/UX refers to how easily users can interact with a product or interface. It focuses on creating intuitive, efficient, and enjoyable experiences by ensuring the design is user-friendly, accessible, and meets the user's needs. Good usability leads to higher satisfaction, reduced frustration, and increased product success.

1 - Learnability

2 - Efficiency

3 - Memorability

4 - Error Prevetion

5 - Satisfaction
