5 concepts of Usability!

Usability in UI/UX refers to how easily users can interact with a product or interface. It focuses on creating intuitive, efficient, and enjoyable experiences by ensuring the design is user-friendly, accessible, and meets the user's needs. Good usability leads to higher satisfaction, reduced frustration, and increased product success.

1 - Learnability

2 - Efficiency

3 - Memorability

4 - Error Prevetion

5 - Satisfaction

Colors Theory!

Color theory is the study of how colors work together and the emotions they trigger. Warm colors—like red, orange, and yellow—tend to evoke feelings of warmth, passion, and energy. On the other hand, cool colors—such as blue, green, and purple—are calming, symbolizing peace, stability, and relaxation. Colors also carry cultural meanings; for instance, red can represent love or danger, while blue is often associated with trust and serenity. By understanding color theory, we can use colors more effectively in design and communication to influence moods and perceptions.
